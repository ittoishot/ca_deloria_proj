Which is better? Online shopping or Traditional shopping?

By Mark Joseph A. Deloria



Online Shopping

Online shopping allows consumers to buy the product they want over the internet. It is a form of electronic commerce. Consumers can search different products over the internet by using shopping apps of thru surfing shopping websites. Websites and apps related to online shopping have filters so that consumers can easily find what they need. As for the payment method, they can use debit cards, credit cards, e-wallets, or they could also cash on delivery.



Shopping online has its pros and cons:


Pros Cons
Convenience Risk of Fraud
No crowds Cannot see the product physically
Product reviews No sales assistance
Easy price comparisons Product might not be what you expected
More variety Return fees


Traditional Shopping

Traditional shoping is buying the product directly from the store. The consumer goes to the mall they like and check each and every store for the product they are looking for. Products in the store can be checked and inspect first by the buyer before buying it to ensure it's not damaged or there are no problems. The method of payment when shopping in a traditional way is paying at the cashier. Traditional shopping lets the buyers bring the product the same day as they purchased it.



Just like Online shopping, Traditional shopping also has its pros and cons:


Pros Cons
You can try on the clothes Crowds
Can inspect the product Takes a lot of time
You get the product immeadiately Comparing prices is hard
Lesser chance of being scammed Store might not have the product you want
You can see the design Must travel a distance first before reaching the store



My Opinion

Now that we are in a pandemic, most people do online shopping than traditional shopping. But for me, as long as we follow health standard protocols and are vaccinated when going out, I still prefer doing traditional shopping. Why? First is because I can see the product and inspect it before buying and I'm sure about the look of what I bought unlike in online shopping, there are times that the product has malfunctions. Second, I don't have to wait for several days for the product to arrive, there are also times that the delivery is very late. But when shopping traditionally you get the product immeadiately and take it home. Lastly, I have lesser chances of getting scammed or getting my credit card info stolen. It is because when you do online shopping you either pay on delivery or use credit cards of e-wallets and for me I'm worried about my info getting stolen. Those are three reasons why I prefer traditional shopping over online shopping. But it's up to you which method you go for.
